Return & Refund Policy

We strive to provide the best products and services to our customers. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please read our return policy below.

1. Eligibility for Returns

You can return a product within 7 days of delivery if it is unused, unopened, and in its original packaging.

2. Refund Process

Once we receive your returned product, we will inspect it and initiate the refund within 5–7 business days.

3. Non-Returnable Items

Personal care products, cosmetics, and opened food items cannot be returned due to hygiene reasons.

4. Contact Us

If you have any return queries, please contact us at support@omsai.com.
